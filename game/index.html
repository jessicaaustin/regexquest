Regex Help

Defeat the zombie attack by matching the infected words.

To match, type a pattern in the first box, and modifiers in the second box, then hit <enter>.

Available modifiers:

  • /g -- global matching
  • /i -- case insensitive
  • /m -- multi-line matching
